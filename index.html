My Workflow

How I Work

From blank page to top-performing creative — here's how every playable gets built.

💡 01
Ideation & Research
Market analysis, competitor audits, hook generation. I find what resonates with the audience before a single line of code is written.
📄 02
Spec Writing
Full GDD: mechanics, tutorial flow, UI layout, end-card strategy. Everything the dev team needs — zero guesswork.
🎯 03
Team Briefing
Clear creative briefs for devs, artists and QA. I stay hands-on through production to keep the build true to the vision.
📊 04
Launch & Iterate
A/B testing, KPI tracking, data-driven iteration. Performance data feeds directly back into the next concept cycle.
